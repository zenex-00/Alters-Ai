Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans.

How do monthly interactions work?

An interaction is defined as any exchange between your digital alter and a user. This includes text messages, voice conversations, or any other form of communication. Each plan includes a specific number of interactions per month, and additional interactions can be purchased if needed.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new features will be available immediately, and you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What's the difference between personality modeling levels?

Basic personality modeling captures fundamental aspects of your communication style and preferences. Advanced modeling includes more nuanced understanding of context, tone, and expertise areas. Premium modeling offers the most sophisticated representation, including emotional intelligence, specialized knowledge domains, and adaptive learning capabilities.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 14 days, you can request a full refund. After this period, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our support team for assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through our payment providers.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for our Basic and Professional plans. The trial includes limited access to features so you can experience the platform before committing. No credit card is required to start your trial. Enterprise plans include a personalized demo instead of a self-service trial.
