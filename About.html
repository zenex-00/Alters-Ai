Our Story

Founded in 2022, alters.ai emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: what if everyone could create an intelligent digital version of themselves that could handle tasks, communicate, and represent them online?

Our founders, a team of AI researchers and digital identity experts, recognized that as our lives become increasingly digital, the need for authentic representation in virtual spaces would become essential.

Today, we're at the forefront of AI-powered digital identity technology, helping individuals and businesses create intelligent digital twins that maintain their unique voice, expertise, and personality.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and organizations with intelligent digital identities that authentically represent them in the digital world, enhancing human potential without replacing human connection.

Our Vision

A world where everyone has access to intelligent digital representation, enabling them to scale their presence, share their knowledge, and connect across boundaries of time and space.

Our Journey

From a small startup to a leader in digital identity technology, our path has been defined by innovation and growth.

2022

Foundation

alters.ai was founded by Elena Rodriguez and David Chen with a vision to create intelligent digital twins that could authentically represent individuals online.

2023

First Product Launch

Released our beta platform to select users, allowing them to create their first digital alters with basic communication capabilities.

2024

Series A Funding

Secured $25 million in Series A funding to expand our team and enhance our AI technology, with a focus on natural language processing and personality modeling.

2025

Global Expansion

Launched our full platform globally with advanced features including multimodal communication, task automation, and enterprise solutions for businesses.

Our Technology & Ethical Approach

We're committed to developing AI that respects human autonomy, privacy, and dignity. Our technology is built with ethical considerations at its core.

Advanced AI Technology

  • Natural Language Processing

    Our proprietary NLP models understand context, tone, and personal communication styles to create authentic digital representations.

  • Personality Modeling

    We've developed advanced algorithms that capture the nuances of individual personalities, preferences, and expertise.

  • Multimodal Communication

    Our platform integrates text, voice, and visual elements to create comprehensive digital representations.

Ethical AI Framework

  • Transparency

    We're committed to clear disclosure of AI involvement in all interactions, ensuring users always know when they're engaging with a digital alter.

  • Data Privacy

    We maintain the highest standards of data protection, giving users full control over their information and how their digital alters are used.

  • Human Oversight

    Our AI systems are designed with appropriate human oversight and control, ensuring technology enhances rather than replaces human connection.
